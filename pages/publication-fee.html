Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really no publication fee?

Yes, absolutely zero. IJACM does not charge any Article Processing Charges (APC), submission fees, or any kind of publication fees for authors from any country.

When would I have to pay?

Never. There is no payment required at any stage — neither during submission, nor after acceptance, nor for publication.

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs whatsoever. We do not charge for color figures, extra pages, formatting, or processing.

Will quality be compromised?

Not at all. Our peer-review process remains rigorous and double-blind. Editorial decisions are based solely on scholarly merit.

Do I still get a DOI and indexing?

Absolutely. Every accepted paper receives a unique Crossref DOI, and the journal is indexed in Google Scholar and other major databases.

How to submit my paper for free?

Simply submit your manuscript through our online submission system or email to editor@ijacm.com. There are no charges at any step.
