Frequently Asked Questions — No APC Policy

Is there really no publication fee?

Yes, absolutely zero. As per our new policy, International Journal of Advanced Computing and Mechanical Systems (IJACM) does not charge any Article Processing Charges (APC), submission fees, or any kind of publication fees for authors from any country.

When would I have to pay?

Never. There is no payment required at any stage — neither during submission, nor after acceptance, nor for publication. The entire process is completely free of charge.

Are there any hidden costs?

No hidden costs whatsoever. We do not charge for color figures, extra pages, formatting, or processing. Our open access model is funded through institutional partnerships and sponsorships, not author fees.

Will quality be compromised due to no APC?

Not at all. Our peer-review process remains rigorous and double-blind. Editorial decisions are based solely on scholarly merit. We are committed to maintaining high-quality standards while removing financial barriers.

Do I still get a DOI and indexing?

Absolutely. Every accepted paper receives a unique Crossref DOI, and the journal is indexed in Google Scholar and other major databases. Free publication does not affect indexing or discoverability.

How to submit my paper for free?

Simply submit your manuscript through our online submission system or email to editor@ijacm.com. There are no charges at any step. Your work will be reviewed and published open access at zero cost.
