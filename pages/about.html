About IJACM - International Research Journal

IJACM is an international, peer-reviewed, open-access journal focusing on advanced computing technologies and mechanical systems research. We provide a platform for high-quality interdisciplinary scholarship in engineering and computational innovation.

Our Mission & Academic Vision

Our vision is to bridge the gap between theoretical computing and practical mechanical engineering. We provide a global platform for innovative research in computational methods, intelligent algorithms, and system-level applications.

Our mission is to accelerate scientific discovery by publishing high-quality research that advances both computing and mechanical engineering disciplines, while maintaining the highest standards of peer review and ethical publishing.

Open Access Publishing

Open Access

Immediate, unrestricted access to research. No paywalls or subscription fees for the global scientific community.

CC BY 4.0

Authors retain copyright with full attribution. This ensures maximum dissemination and reuse of mechanical systems research.

Global Indexing

Permanent online accessibility. Indexed in Google Scholar, Zenodo, and Semantic Scholar for high visibility.

Academic Quality Standards

  • Single-Blind Peer Review: Unbiased evaluation by senior experts in advanced computing.
  • COPE Ethical Integrity: Strict adherence to COPE guidelines and global publication ethics.
  • Plagiarism Screening: Similarity index must be below 15% using professional screening tools.
  • Rigorous Technical Review: All manuscripts undergo thorough editorial review for engineering precision.
