Aim and Scope of IJACM

The aim of International Journal of Advanced Computing and Mechanical Systems (IJACM) is to promote high-quality research and provide a global platform for scholars, researchers and professionals to publish innovative research findings.

Scope of the Journal

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Information Technology
Primary Objectives
Advance integration of computing & mechanical systems
Promote intelligent algorithms & computational methods
Facilitate academia-industry knowledge exchange
Supports advanced engineering research

  • Digital twins & Cyber-physical systems
  • High-performance computing
  • Cloud & Edge computing applications
